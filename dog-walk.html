Dog Walk

Pet sitting and dog walking app that provides trusted pet care.

Overview

In the beginning, service had two simple app versions — one for dog owners and one for dog walkers. I started to collaborate with the development team to improve and evolve the second app. It provides dog walkers and dog sitters an access to orders from pet owners. I'm responsible for user experience, visual design, ux research and prototyping. Application is available for Android and iOS.
