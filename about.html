About me

I am a digital product designer with more than 8 years of experience. I have a passion for visual, interaction as well as motion design and enjoy creating user-centric experiences that help businesses meet their goals. I have a diverse background in designing mobile apps, websites and online games. My workflow is based on research, visual and experience design together with usability testing. I currently work at Access Softek, helping to build and evolve mobile banking services. Previously, I worked on the consumer mobile experience for a pet-sitting app at Dog-Walk and led design process at AnyTalk — a secure cross-platform messaging app. You can find more details about my experience in my résumé.
